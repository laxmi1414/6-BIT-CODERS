Aadhaar Link ≠ DBT Enabled

Learn how to activate DBT and receive scholarships, pensions, and subsidies directly into your account.

Difference Between Aadhaar-Linked and DBT-Enabled Accounts

Feature Aadhaar Linked DBT Enabled
Purpose Only identity verification Direct transfer of government benefits
Receives subsidy/scholarship ❌ ✅
Required for schemes ❌ ✅
Example Only linked for KYC Benefits credited directly (LPG, Pension, etc.)

How to Enable DBT

1️⃣ Visit your bank branch or online banking portal.
2️⃣ Ensure your Aadhaar is linked with your bank account.
3️⃣ Ask for DBT Activation (Direct Benefit Transfer).
4️⃣ Verify your DBT status at dbtbharat.gov.in.
5️⃣ Start receiving benefits directly in your account!
